原胚設計

成立於2021年的原胚設計,希望能創造不受限於固有思維的空間,賦予空 間內涵及永續,以功能需求為前提,讓客戶享受最棒的舒適體驗。 追求空間意義內涵，感受事物的本質 -「原」。 醞釀打磨，將每件作品不斷打磨修正，最後臻至完美-「胚」。

Central Idea

Yuanpei interior DecorationCo., Ltd. was originally founded by a young man who loves interior design. After being cultivated by the industry, he has accumulated professional qualities and personal characteristics. Starting from the drawing, we care about the weight and thickness of each line. The picture should be clean and concise, clearly expressing the construction details and dimensional logic. In terms of design, we integrate the needs of customers, integrate design elements into it, and create exclusive works for users.
